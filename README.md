# EDtechStartupAnalysisDashboardbyusingPowerBi


Excited to share a recent project where I dove into online course analytics! 📊📚
I explored course data across categories, languages, and audience reach to uncover insights into instructor distribution, viewer preferences, and content availability.
🔹 Instructors by Category : Arts & Humanities leads with over 15% of instructors, followed by Social Sciences and Physical Science & Engineering.
🔹 Viewers by Language : English dominates, but multilingual subtitles (Spanish, French, Japanese, etc.) show strong global engagement.
🔹 Course Availability by Language : French, Russian, and Portuguese courses show varied counts, highlighting regional content gaps and opportunities.
🔹 Subtitle Coverage : Academic and Professional Development courses offer the widest language support, enhancing accessibility worldwide.
This analysis helped visualize how course offerings align with learner demand
and where there’s room to grow 🌍💡
